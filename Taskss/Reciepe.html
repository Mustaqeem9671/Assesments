Recipe for Paneer Masala

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 cup Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup Tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup Capsicum, diced
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Cream
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds. Sauté until they splutter.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Stir in the pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add diced capsicum and cook until it becomes tender.
  6. Lower the heat, add cubed paneer, and mix gently. Allow paneer to absorb the flavors.
  7. Pour fresh cream and sprinkle garam masala. Mix well and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
